Satellite Services

Our AI model offers a comprehensive solution for farmers seeking to boost their yields through the utilization of NDVI and NDWI techniques. Step one involves providing farmers with detailed guidance on the necessary steps, equipment, and chemicals required for optimizing their agricultural practices. By leveraging NDVI and NDWI data, our model offers insights into crop health, soil moisture, and nutrient requirements, enabling farmers to make informed decisions.

Later, the model takes it a step further by suggesting specific items to purchase directly from our E-commerce platform. This feature streamlines the procurement process, ensuring that farmers have easy access to the recommended equipment and chemicals. By seamlessly connecting data-driven recommendations with convenient online shopping, we empower farmers to implement efficient strategies and enhance their agricultural productivity while simplifying the purchasing process through our ecommerce site.
